# Arena Hub
## Sports Arena Booking and Management Platform

Version: 1.0
Status: Development Phase
Methodology: Agile (Iterative & Incremental)

---

# 1. Project Overview

Arena Hub is a centralized sports arena booking and management platform that connects players with sports facility owners through a mobile and web-based system. The platform aims to eliminate the traditional manual booking process, which often relies on phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and personal contacts.

The system provides real-time arena discovery, slot booking, online payments, automated booking management, equipment rental, reviews and ratings, AI-powered arena recommendations, and administrative monitoring through a centralized platform.

Arena Hub consists of three main stakeholders:

1. Players (Mobile Application)
2. Arena Owners (Web Dashboard)
3. Administrators (Web Dashboard)

The system will be accessible through React Native mobile applications for Android and iOS, while Arena Owners and Administrators will use a Next.js web dashboard. FastAPI will serve as the backend API layer, MongoDB Atlas will be used as the primary database, Redis will prevent double bookings through distributed locking, and WebSockets will provide real-time updates.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Sports activities are becoming increasingly popular, but sports facility booking remains largely manual. Players often have to call arena owners, send WhatsApp messages, or physically visit venues to check availability and make reservations.

This manual approach creates several problems:

- Difficulty finding suitable arenas
- Lack of real-time slot availability
- Double booking conflicts
- Inefficient booking management
- Limited payment options
- Poor customer experience
- Lack of centralized booking records
- Inefficient arena management

Arena owners face similar challenges when managing schedules, bookings, customer records, pricing, and payments manually.

There is currently no unified platform that efficiently connects players and arena owners while providing real-time booking capabilities and digital payment solutions.

---

# 3. Proposed Solution

Arena Hub provides a centralized platform where users can:

- Search sports arenas
- View arena details
- Check real-time slot availability
- Book slots instantly
- Pay online
- Receive booking confirmations
- View booking history
- Submit reviews and ratings

Arena Owners can:

- Register their facilities
- Manage courts
- Manage slots
- Manage pricing
- Manage equipment rentals
- Track bookings
- Monitor revenue

Administrators can:

- Verify arenas
- Manage users
- Monitor bookings
- Handle complaints
- Generate reports

The system uses Redis locking to prevent double bookings and WebSockets to synchronize slot availability in real time.

---

# 4. Project Objectives

The objectives of Arena Hub are:

- To build a centralized sports arena booking platform.
- To provide real-time slot availability information.
- To prevent double bookings using distributed locking.
- To enable secure online payments.
- To simplify arena management for owners.
- To provide AI-based arena recommendations.
- To support equipment rental functionality.
- To provide reviews and ratings.
- To improve user experience for players and owners.
- To provide administrative control and monitoring.

Updated Payment Flow
When a user books a slot, the system should provide two payment options:

Option 1: Full Payment

User pays 100% booking amount online.

Booking is confirmed immediately.

Receipt is generated automatically.

Option 2: Advance Payment

User pays only a predefined advance amount (e.g., 20%, 30%, or 50%).

Remaining amount is paid at the arena on arrival.

Booking is confirmed immediately after advance payment.

Receipt shows:

Total Amount

Advance Paid

Remaining Balance

Payment Approval
Similar to Daewoo and Faisal Movers:

User completes payment through Stripe, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, or Bank Transfer.

Payment gateway confirms transaction automatically.

System automatically approves booking.

Digital receipt is generated instantly.

No manual approval is required.

Arena Owner Payment Settings
Arena owners should be able to configure:

Advance Payment Percentage

Full Payment Requirement (Optional)

Refund Policy

Cancellation Policy

Example:

Arena A
Advance Required: 30%

OR

Full Payment Required: Yes
Refund Policy
I recommend making it configurable by the arena owner.

Default Policy
Cancellation > 6 hours before booking
→ Refund Allowed

Cancellation < 6 hours before booking
→ No Refund
Custom Policy
Arena Owner can set:

24 Hours Before = 100% Refund

12 Hours Before = 50% Refund

6 Hours Before = No Refund
or

No Refunds Allowed
Additional Database Fields
In Booking Collection:

{
  "paymentType": "FULL | ADVANCE",
  "totalAmount": 5000,
  "advanceAmount": 1500,
  "remainingAmount": 3500,
  "paymentStatus": "PAID | PARTIALLY_PAID",
  "refundEligible": true
}
In Arena Collection:

{
  "advancePercentage": 30,
  "refundPolicy": {
    "refundBeforeHours": 6,
    "refundPercentage": 100
  }
}
This approach is practical for Pakistan's sports arenas because many owners prefer receiving a booking commitment through an advance payment while allowing the remaining amount to be paid on-site.
